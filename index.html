A wandering I will go

I"m either on a plane and excited, or watching planes and jealous. Being raised in a place that you knew, from the earliest cognitive grasps, that you didn't belong set up a future of wandering. I love being home, but I love being out of my element, taking in new culture, language, & traditions. Global peace would be greatly enhanced if more of us purposfully exposed ourselves to that which is not like us. Do it.

Push your boundries - both emotional and physical. Treat your senses - taste, sight, sound and smell. And do it soon!

Adult on-set passion for code

After leaving a career in finance, and working with an execitive coach, I was 'diagnosed' as a 'creative-type'. Sounds great, now. At the time, it felt like a terminal diagnosis. I didn't know a thing about creativity. I was a CFO! Thanks to the steady hand of a talented coach, I enrolled in a Ruby/Rails bootcamp in Boston. After 12 weeks I discovered not only that I was creative, but that my destiny was in Front End Development.

A discovered passion at 36 years of age. Better late than never.

The ever balanced Libra

I approach the on-line experience from both the right and left-brain. I learn from both construction and deconstruction. These balances have been the guiding principles in my life and are so beautifully applied to my educational endeavor in code.

For every action there is a reaction - either physical or emotional. By remembering the importance of balance, one can find and enjoy harmony in life. I'm advantaged by being born under the Libra sign so the stars have given me a leg-up.
